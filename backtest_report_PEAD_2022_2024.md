- **Guidance raise filter** was NOT applied due to yfinance data limitations. All results reflect 'EPS beat only' criterion. Adding a guidance filter would reduce trade count but likely improve quality.

## 8. Limitations

1. **Survivorship bias**: Current S&P 500 only — companies removed during 2022–2024 are excluded.
2. **Guidance raise unverified**: yfinance does not provide guidance data reliably. Tested 'EPS beat' only.
3. **Fill assumptions**: Entry at open 2 days after earnings. Real fills may be worse (gap-up opens).
4. **Earnings dates**: yfinance earnings_history dates can be off by 1 day vs actual announcement time (pre/post market).
5. **No transaction costs**: Commissions, spread, and slippage are not modeled (~$5–$20 per round trip).
6. **No portfolio constraints**: Simulated as if each trade is independent; in reality concurrent positions compete for capital.
7. **Split/dividend adjustment**: yfinance auto_adjust=True handles splits but may introduce small errors on exact stop levels.
8. **Thesis break exits**: Not modeled (requires next-quarter earnings data matched back to open positions).

## 9. Recommendation

**CAUTIOUS CONTINUE.** The strategy has a marginal edge.

Results are sensitive to assumptions and survivorship bias. Before scaling, validate on out-of-sample data (2025) and add guidance-raise filtering. Consider tightening the EPS surprise threshold (e.g., beat > 5%) to improve quality over quantity.
